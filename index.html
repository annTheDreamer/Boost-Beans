Mocha Madness

Delight in the perfect blend of rich espresso made from Ethiopian Yirgacheffe beans, velvety chocolate, and steamed almond milk, topped with a dollop of dairy-free whipped cream. A true indulgence that satisfies your sweet cravings without compromising on flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (Ethiopian Yirgacheffe)
  • 1 tablespoon of high-quality cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • Dairy-free whipped cream (for topping)
  • Cocoa nibs or chocolate shavings (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Start by brewing a shot of espresso using Ethiopian Yirgacheffe beans. Make sure to extract the espresso according to your machine's instructions. The medium roast profile of these beans will complement the chocolate flavors perfectly.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the cocoa powder and granulated sugar. Mix them well to create a cocoa-sugar blend. Adjust the sugar quantity based on your preference for sweetness.
  3. Steam the unsweetened almond milk using a milk frother or steam wand until it's nicely heated and frothy. The almond milk's creaminess will contribute to the velvety texture of the drink.
  4. In a serving mug, add the prepared shot of espresso. Stir in the cocoa-sugar blend until it's fully dissolved in the espresso.
  5. Gently pour the steamed almond milk into the mug, holding back the foam with a spoon to ensure a smooth and even distribution of milk and foam.
  6. For a touch of indulgence, top your Mocha Madness with a generous dollop of dairy-free whipped cream. This adds a delightful creaminess to the drink and enhances its overall texture.
  7. Optional: To make the presentation even more inviting, sprinkle a few cocoa nibs or chocolate shavings on top of the whipped cream. This not only adds visual appeal but also a satisfying crunch.
  8. Give the drink a gentle stir to combine all the flavors before sipping.
  9. Enjoy your homemade Mocha Madness, a comforting and luxurious treat that brings together the best of coffee and chocolate.

Caramel Dream

Savor the sweet indulgence of smooth caramel sauce, a double shot of espresso made from Colombian Supremo beans, and frothy milk, finished with a drizzle of caramel syrup. An exquisite treat for your taste buds that adds a touch of decadence to your coffee moments.

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of Colombian Supremo espresso (double shot)
  • 2 tablespoons smooth caramel sauce
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Caramel syrup (for drizzling)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
  • Crushed caramel candies (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Brew two shots of double espresso using Colombian Supremo beans with a dark roast profile. This robust base will perfectly balance the sweetness of caramel.
  2. Warm the smooth caramel sauce gently by microwaving it for a few seconds or using a double boiler.
  3. Froth the whole milk using a milk frother or steam wand until it's creamy and velvety.
  4. In a serving mug, pour in the double shot of espresso.
  5. Stir in the warmed caramel sauce until it's fully incorporated into the espresso.
  6. Gently pour the frothed whole milk into the mug, allowing the foam to rise above the rim.
  7. Drizzle caramel syrup over the milk foam for an extra layer of caramel flavor and a visually appealing touch.
  8. Optional: Add a dollop of whipped cream on top for added richness and indulgence.
  9. If desired, garnish the drink with crushed caramel candies. These will add a delightful crunch and enhance the caramel experience.
  10. Enjoy your homemade Caramel Dream, a delightful and satisfying indulgence that elevates your coffee experience.

Vanilla Bliss

Experience the comforting embrace of vanilla-infused coffee, made from Costa Rican Tarrazu beans, steamed oat milk, and a sprinkle of cinnamon on top. A warm and delightful start to your day that exudes pure bliss with every sip.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Costa Rican Tarrazu coffee (brewed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup oat milk (steamed)
  • Ground cinnamon (for sprinkling)

Instructions

  1. Brew one cup of Costa Rican Tarrazu coffee using a light roast profile. The light roast will bring out the beans' unique flavors, enhancing the vanilla notes.
  2. While the coffee is brewing, steam oat milk until it's warm and frothy using a milk frother or steam wand.
  3. In a serving mug, pour the brewed Costa Rican Tarrazu coffee.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract, infusing the coffee with its soothing aroma and flavor.
  5. Gently pour the steamed oat milk into the mug, allowing the foam to form a creamy layer on top.
  6. Sprinkle a pinch of ground cinnamon over the milk foam. The warm spice will complement the vanilla and coffee flavors.
  7. Give the drink a gentle stir to ensure that the vanilla and cinnamon are evenly distributed.
  8. Adjust the vanilla extract and cinnamon to suit your preferred level of flavor intensity.
  9. Enjoy Vanilla Bliss as a calming and delightful way to start your day, or as a soothing treat to unwind in the afternoon.
